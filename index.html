About Me

Born in San Antonio, Texas and raised in many different places such as Germany, Georgia, Texas, Illinois, and Missouri, I was made to understand different cultures and different people. I am grateful for having this experience as a child as it allowed me to broaden my knowledge of the world I live in which eventually left me more curious than ever. One thing it did not help, however, was my education. Constantly being on the move took a toll due to how different teaching was structured in each of the places I lived. This led me to be behind in my studies come high school, especially in math. I originally disliked the subject, but as I became better towards the latter half of my high school career, I was really starting to enjoy it. I wanted to use math in a useful way, so I decided to take a computer science class my junior year of school. This class is what made me more invested in the study of computer science.
